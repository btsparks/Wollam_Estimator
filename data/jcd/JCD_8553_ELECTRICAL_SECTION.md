# JOB COST DATA (JCD) — 8553 RTK SPD PUMP STATION
## ELECTRICAL SECTION

---

## 1. SCOPE SUMMARY

Electrical scope for the SPD Pump Station including complete electrical systems for a ~35,000 SF industrial pump station building. Wollam held the prime contract for the electrical subcontractor and self-performed excavation and concrete support work for duct banks, ground grids, and equipment pads.

**Contracting Strategy:**
- **Electrical Subcontractor (Hunt Electric - 4100):** Complete electrical systems including power distribution, lighting, controls, instrumentation
- **Wollam Self-Perform:** Excavation/backfill for duct banks, ground grid, equipment pads; concrete for duct bank encasement and pads
- **Owner-Furnished Equipment:** Transformer, ATS (tracked in 5500)

**Building Parameters:**
- Building Footprint: ~35,000 SF
- Building Type: Pre-Engineered Metal Building (PEMB) for SPD Pump Station
- Eave Height: ~40 ft average
- 18.5-Ton Bridge Crane: Integral to structure

## 8. LESSONS LEARNED

### What Went Well

1. **Self-performed support work (-44% MH)** - Excavation and concrete for electrical came in significantly under budget. Crews were efficient with equipment support.

2. **Transformer/ATS on budget** - Owner-furnished equipment came in as expected.

### What Would We Do Differently

1. **Electrical sub contingency** - Budget went 12.5% over. For future complex industrial electrical, include 10-15% contingency or negotiate firmer pricing.

2. **Change order tracking** - Better documentation of electrical scope changes to understand variance drivers.
