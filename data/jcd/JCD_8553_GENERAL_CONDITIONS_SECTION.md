## 9. KEY LESSONS LEARNED

### 9.1 Management & Supervision

1. **Budget $2,500/day for management** (PM + Super + Admin) on similar industrial projects
2. **Verify owner safety requirements** before bidding dedicated safety staff - RTK provided site safety
3. **Budget 2-3x training hours** for mine site work - RTKC training was 380% over budget
4. **Include equipment R&M allowance** of $5K/month for extended projects

### 9.2 Survey

1. **Digital Earth provided excellent service** at ~$18K/month during active phases
2. **Survey support tapers after concrete/steel** - minimal during mechanical/electrical
3. **Budget $100-150K** for similar complexity project
4. **Daily presence critical** during foundation excavation and concrete layout

### 9.3 QC Testing

1. **QC cost ~0.65% of actual job cost** when contractor-provided
2. **Heaviest testing during earthwork backfill and concrete** phases
3. **Budget $12-15/CY for concrete testing** (cylinders, slump, air, temp, density)
4. **Budget $0.50-1.00/CY for compaction testing**
5. **Full-time inspector required** during all concrete pours and backfill operations

### 9.4 Site Work

1. **Dust control may be owner-provided** on mine sites - verify before bidding full water truck
2. **Snow removal highly variable** - budget conservatively but expect variability
3. **Access road maintenance** integrated with earthwork operations

### 9.5 Insurance & Overhead

1. **GL Insurance runs ~0.70-0.75%** of job cost
2. **Overhead allocation varies** by company structure - typically 2-3%
3. **Standby crews rarely used** if absorbed in normal operations
